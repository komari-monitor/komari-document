# Other Community Projects

This page is for related tools, integrations, scripts, and examples maintained by the community.

When evaluating a community project:

- Check the source repository and license.
- Verify compatibility with your Komari version.
- Test in a non-production environment first.
- Avoid giving unnecessary privileges to third-party scripts.

See the Chinese community page for the current project list: [Other community projects](/community/other).
